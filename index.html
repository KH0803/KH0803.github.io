Kukhyeon Kim 김국현

Ph.D. in Educational Technology
Postdoctoral Researcher · BK21 Flourishing Education · Chonnam National University

I am an educational technologist specializing in the design and development of immersive XR/VR/AR simulations for learning. My research focuses on multimodal physiological measurement—integrating eye tracking, EEG, and facial expression analysis—to understand how learners think, feel, and engage within simulated environments. I also explore digital human interactions, learning experience design (LXD), and AI-integrated teacher education.
